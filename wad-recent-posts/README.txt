Simple and clean widget for showing recent posts list. It also has shortcode feature.

== Description ==

With this plugin you can show a widget containing most recent posts list. With it's shortcode feature, it will help you to show the most recent post in a specific category by placing the shortcode anywhere on your website, such as posts and pages.
